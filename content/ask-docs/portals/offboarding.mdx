## Overview

**My exit checklist** at `/{locale}/p/{portalSlug}/employee/offboarding` shows tasks HR assigned when your offboarding case is active. Complete each item you are responsible for; HR tracks overall progress in HRM.

## Before you start

- Employee portal session with audience `employee`.
- The checklist appears only after HR **initiates** offboarding for your employee record.

## Complete checklist tasks

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open My exit checklist

    From the employee portal, open **Offboarding** (exit checklist) in the section navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Review tasks

    Each row describes what you must return, acknowledge, or confirm. Due dates may appear when configured.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Mark tasks complete

    Use the complete action on tasks assigned to you. Some tasks may require evidence or HR verification before they close.

    <Callout type="info" title="HR-owned tasks">
      Items owned by HR or IT may stay open until those teams confirm in HRM. You cannot skip system-enforced gates.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Authority and audit

Task completion audits with `erp.hrm.*` offboarding actions and `metadata.surface = employee_portal` where applicable.

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Checklist is empty">
    Offboarding may not be initiated yet, or your portal access may have been revoked early. Contact HR.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Complete button is disabled">
    A dependency task may be open, or HR may require manual verification first.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
