## Overview

**My signatures** at `/{locale}/p/{portalSlug}/employee/signatures` lists documents awaiting your signature and opens a **ceremony** at `/employee/signatures/{token}` to submit or decline. Ceremonies are tenant-bound and time-limited.

## Before you start

- Employee portal session with audience `employee`.
- Completing a ceremony may require **recent authentication (step-up)**.

## Sign or decline

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open My signatures

    From the employee portal, open **Signatures** in the section navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Open a ceremony

    Select a pending row to open the ceremony page. Read the document summary and legal copy before acting.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Complete step-up when prompted

    Submitting or declining may call `requireRecentAuthStepUp` — re-authenticate if your session is stale.

    <Callout type="warn" title="Tier A action">
      Signature completion is irreversible for compliance. Decline only when you intend to reject the document; HR may reissue a new ceremony.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Submit or decline

    Follow the ceremony controls to **submit** your signature or **decline** with a reason when supported.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Authority and audit

- Ceremony tokens map to a single party on an org-owned signature request.
- Successful submit/decline audits after commit with `erp.hrm.*` signature actions and `metadata.surface = employee_portal` where applicable.

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Step-up keeps failing">
    Sign out and sign in again, then retry within the step-up time window. Contact IT if MFA or session policies block completion.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ceremony link expired">
    Ask HR to reissue the signature request from HRM.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
