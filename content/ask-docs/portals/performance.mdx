## Overview

**My performance** at `/{locale}/p/{portalSlug}/employee/performance` lists KPI goals assigned to you and opens read-only detail at `/employee/performance/goals/{goalId}`. You cannot edit goal targets or review scores from the portal — managers own the review cycle in HRM.

## Before you start

- Employee portal session with audience `employee`.
- Goals appear only after HR or your manager publishes them to your employee record.

## View goals

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open My performance

    From the employee portal, open **Performance** in the section navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Review the goal list

    Scan period, status, and progress indicators shown for each assigned goal.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Open goal detail

    Select a goal to read description, metrics, and manager comments available to employees.

    <Callout type="warn" title="Read-only surface">
      The portal does not accept goal edits or self-ratings unless a future release adds an explicit employee action. Use HRM or manager conversation for disputes.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Portal vs HRM

| Action | Employee portal | HRM |
| --- | --- | --- |
| View assigned goals | Yes | Yes (full) |
| Edit goals / finalize review | — | Yes (manager / HR) |

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="No goals listed">
    Your review cycle may not be published yet. Contact your manager or HR.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Progress looks stale">
    Refresh the page after manager updates. Lag may occur until the next sync from HRM.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
