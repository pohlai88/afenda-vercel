## Overview

**My attendance** at `/{locale}/p/{portalSlug}/employee/attendance` shows your attendance history and lets you submit **correction requests** when a punch or day record is wrong. Managers resolve corrections in HRM; the portal does not edit attendance rows directly.

## Before you start

- Employee portal session with audience `employee`.
- Correction requests require a reason and the dates or entries you believe are incorrect.

## View history and request a correction

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open My attendance

    From the employee portal, open **Attendance** in the section navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Review your history

    Scan recent days and statuses. Approved **leave** for a date should suppress anomaly flags for that day.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Submit a correction request

    Use the correction form to describe the issue (missing punch, wrong in/out time, etc.) and submit.

    HR or your manager processes the request in HRM → Attendance. You receive the outcome through normal org notification channels.

    <Callout type="info" title="Portal vs Workbench">
      The portal only **requests** corrections. Authoritative attendance truth is updated in HRM after review.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Portal vs HRM

| Action | Employee portal | HRM |
| --- | --- | --- |
| View own attendance | Yes | Yes (broader) |
| Submit correction request | Yes | — |
| Approve / apply correction | — | Yes |

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="A day still shows an anomaly during approved leave">
    Allow time for leave approval to sync, or submit a correction request with the leave reference.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My correction was rejected">
    Read the reason returned by the approver and resubmit with clearer evidence if policy allows.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
