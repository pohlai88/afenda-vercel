## Overview

**My documents** at `/{locale}/p/{portalSlug}/employee/documents` lists documents shared with you and lets you **request** new HR documents (contracts, letters, certificates). Uploads and expiry tracking for compliance vault files are managed in HRM; the portal focuses on self-service requests and downloads you are allowed to see.

## Before you start

- Employee portal session with audience `employee`.
- Some document types require HR to fulfil the request manually after submission.

## Request a document

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open My documents

    From the employee portal, open **Documents** in the section navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Review available files

    Download or open rows your organisation has published to your employee record.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Submit a document request

    Use the request form to choose **document type**, add context, and submit. HR processes the queue in HRM.

    <Callout type="info" title="Vault vs portal">
      Compliance vault uploads and expiry watches are configured in HRM → Documents. The portal does not bypass vault permissions.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Portal vs HRM

| Action | Employee portal | HRM |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Request document | Yes | Fulfil request |
| Upload to compliance vault | — | Yes (permissioned) |
| Expiry notifications | — | Yes (cron / settings) |

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="I cannot see a document HR mentioned">
    The file may not be published to your portal visibility yet, or your role may not include that document type. Contact HR.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My request is still open">
    HR fulfils requests asynchronously. Check status on the documents page or contact HR for SLA expectations.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
