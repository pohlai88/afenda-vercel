## Overview

**My salary advances** at `/{locale}/p/{portalSlug}/employee/advances` lets you request an advance against payroll and cancel requests that have not yet been approved or paid. Amount limits and approval rules are configured in HRM.

## Before you start

- Employee portal session with audience `employee`.
- Your organisation must allow employee-initiated advance requests.

## Request or cancel an advance

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open My advances

    From the employee portal, open **Advances** (or **Salary advances**) in the section navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Submit a request

    Enter the **amount** and any required reason or schedule fields, then submit. Finance or HR approves in HRM.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Cancel pending requests

    Use **Cancel** on rows still awaiting approval. Paid or approved advances must be adjusted by HR in HRM.

    <Callout type="info" title="Payroll linkage">
      Approved advances may reduce net pay on the next posted payroll run — see payslips after payroll closes.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Portal vs HRM

| Action | Employee portal | HRM |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Request advance | Yes | — |
| Approve / pay | — | Yes |
| Cancel pending | Yes | — |

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Request exceeds limit">
    Your org may cap advance amount or frequency. Contact HR for an exception process.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Advance not on payslip yet">
    Wait for the payroll period that includes the advance deduction to be posted and published.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
