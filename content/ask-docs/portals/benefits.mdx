## Overview

**My benefits** at `/{locale}/p/{portalSlug}/employee/benefits` shows open enrollment windows, active enrollments, and actions to **enroll**, **waive**, or record a **life event** when your organisation allows self-service changes.

## Before you start

- Employee portal session with audience `employee`.
- Enrollment may be limited to defined windows — outside the window, only HR can change elections in HRM.

## Manage enrollments

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open My benefits

    From the employee portal, open **Benefits** in the section navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Review plans

    Compare available plans, coverage tiers, and effective dates shown for your employee record.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Enroll, waive, or report a life event

    Follow the on-page actions for the enrollment period. Submit when selections are complete.

    <Callout type="warn" title="Election windows">
      Changes outside an open window may be rejected. Contact HR for qualifying life events not exposed in the portal.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Portal vs HRM

| Action | Employee portal | HRM |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Self-service enroll / waive | When window open | Always (admin) |
| Configure plans | — | Yes |

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Enroll button is disabled">
    The enrollment window may be closed or you may already have a final election for the period.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Life event not listed">
    HR may need to open a manual adjustment in HRM → Benefits.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
