## Overview

**My training** at `/{locale}/p/{portalSlug}/employee/training` lists learning assignments for your employee record and lets you mark progress and submit **feedback** when a module requires it. Course authoring and assignment rules live in HRM.

## Before you start

- Employee portal session with audience `employee`.
- Some assignments may link to external content — follow the launch action on each row.

## Complete assignments

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open My training

    From the employee portal, open **Training** in the section navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Open an assignment

    Select a row to view due date, status, and launch or completion actions.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Submit feedback when prompted

    After completing content, use the feedback form if the assignment requires a rating or comments.

    <Callout type="info" title="Assignments are org-owned">
      Only training assigned to your employee record appears. Contact HR if you expect a course that is missing.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Portal vs HRM

| Action | Employee portal | HRM |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Complete assigned training | Yes | Track / assign |
| Create courses | — | Yes |

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Assignment still shows overdue">
    Complete all required steps and refresh. If status is wrong, contact HR to reconcile in HRM → Training.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="External link does not open">
    Check popup blockers and corporate network policy for the training vendor URL.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
