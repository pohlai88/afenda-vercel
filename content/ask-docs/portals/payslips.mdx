## Overview

**My payslips** at `/{locale}/p/{portalSlug}/employee/payslips` lists payslip documents published for your employee record. Open a row to view detail and download the PDF when your organisation allows it. Amounts respect your locale formatting. This surface is **read-only** — payroll posting happens in HRM.

## Before you start

- Active employee portal session with audience `employee`.
- Payslips appear only after payroll has published documents for your record.

## View and download payslips

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open My payslips

    From the employee portal, open **Payslips** in the section navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Select a period

    Choose a payslip row from the list to open the detail view (`/employee/payslips/{documentId}`).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Download the PDF

    Use the download action on the detail page when enabled. If a period is missing, contact HR — you cannot create or edit payslips from the portal.

    <Callout type="warn" title="Read-only surface">
      Payslips are issued by payroll in HRM. The portal never mutates pay amounts or posting state.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Portal vs HRM

| Action | Employee portal | HRM |
| --- | --- | --- |
| View / download payslip | Yes | Yes (admin) |
| Run payroll / post payslips | — | Yes |

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="No payslips appear in the list">
    Payroll may not have published documents for your employee yet, or your portal access may not be linked to the correct employee row. Contact HR.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Download is disabled">
    Your organisation may restrict employee download. HR can provide the document through the documents vault or another channel.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
