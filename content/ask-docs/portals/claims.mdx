## Overview

**My claims** at `/{locale}/p/{portalSlug}/employee/claims` lets you create reimbursable claims, attach evidence, submit for approval, and cancel drafts or pending rows before they are finalized. Approvers work the queue in HRM; approved unpaid claims may surface in payroll preparation.

## Before you start

- Employee portal session with audience `employee`.
- Evidence files must meet your organisation's upload policy (governed Blob storage).

## Submit and manage claims

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open My claims

    From the employee portal, open **Claims** in the section navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Create a claim

    Choose a **claim type**, enter line items and currency-aware amounts, and attach **evidence** (receipts, invoices). Save as draft or submit immediately.

    <Callout type="info" title="Evidence storage">
      Attachments use the same governed upload contract as the HRM documents vault — not ad-hoc URLs.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Track status

    Open a claim (`/employee/claims/{claimId}`) to see approval state, denials, and approver notes.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Cancel when allowed

    Cancel **draft** or eligible **pending** claims from the list or detail view before approval locks the row.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Portal vs HRM

| Action | Employee portal | HRM |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Submit / cancel own claim | Yes | — |
| Approve / deny | — | Yes |
| Pay via payroll | — | Yes (when configured) |

## Authority and audit

Portal claim actions resolve authority from portal access rows via `getEmployeePortalContext` — not client-supplied org or employee IDs.

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="My claim was denied">
    Read the structured denial reason on the detail page, correct line items or evidence, and submit a new claim if policy allows.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Upload failed">
    Check file type and size limits. Retry with a PDF or image format accepted by your org.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
