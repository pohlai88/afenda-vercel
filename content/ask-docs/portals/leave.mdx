## Overview

Use **My leave** at `/{locale}/p/{portalSlug}/employee/leave` to review balances by leave type, submit requests, and cancel rows that are still pending. Policies, accrual, and manager approval live in HRM; the portal only exposes self-service actions for your own employee record.

## Before you start

- Sign in with a user linked to your **employee** record (or an active portal access grant).
- Your organisation must publish an employee portal with audience `employee`.
- You can **cancel** only requests that have not been approved yet.

## Submit or cancel leave

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open My leave

    From the employee portal, open **Leave** in the section navigation (`/employee/leave` under your portal slug).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Review balances

    The balances card lists each active leave type for the current year. Balances update when requests are **approved** in HRM — not when you submit.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Request leave

    In the request form, choose **leave type**, **start date**, **end date**, optional **half day**, and an optional **reason**, then submit.

    Your manager reviews the request in **HRM → Leave**. Balance is not deducted until approval.

    <Callout type="info" title="Portal vs Workbench">
      Managers approve, reject, or request changes in the Workbench. Employees only submit and cancel their own pending requests here.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Cancel a pending request

    For a row still awaiting approval, use **Cancel**. Approved leave must be reversed by your manager in HRM, not from the portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Portal vs HRM

| Action | Employee portal | HRM (admin / manager) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Submit leave | Yes | — |
| Cancel pending | Yes | — |
| Approve / reject | — | Yes |
| Configure leave types | — | Yes |

## Authority and audit

- `organizationId` and `employeeId` come from `getEmployeePortalContext(portalSlug)` — never from form fields.
- Successful leave mutations audit inside `lib/features/hrm/data/*-mutation.server.ts` with `erp.hrm.*` actions.

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Why can't I cancel approved leave?">
    After approval, balance is committed. Ask your manager to reverse or adjust the request in HRM → Leave.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My balance did not change after I submitted">
    Submission does not deduct balance. Wait for manager approval, then refresh the balances card.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="A leave type is missing from the form">
    Only **active** leave types configured for your organisation appear. Contact HR if you expect a type that is not listed.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
