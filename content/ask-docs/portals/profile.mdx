## Overview

**My profile** at `/{locale}/p/{portalSlug}/employee/profile` links to three self-service areas: **personal**, **emergency contact**, and **banking**. Each sub-route has its own form; banking updates require **step-up authentication**.

## Before you start

- Employee portal session with audience `employee`.
- Only fields your organisation exposes to employees are editable.

## Profile areas

<Tabs items={["Personal", "Emergency", "Banking"]}>
  <Tab value="Personal">
    ### Personal (`/employee/profile/personal`)

    Update name, contact, and other personal fields allowed by policy. Submit the form to save; changes audit as `erp.hrm.employee.profile.update` with `metadata.surface = employee_portal`.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Emergency">
    ### Emergency (`/employee/profile/emergency`)

    Maintain emergency contact name, relationship, and phone numbers. Required fields depend on org configuration.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Banking">
    ### Banking (`/employee/profile/banking`)

    Update payroll banking details. Saving triggers **recent authentication (step-up)** before the mutation runs.

    <Callout type="warn" title="Step-up required">
      Banking profile updates call `requireRecentAuthStepUp` — Tier A sensitive data. Complete MFA or password re-entry when prompted.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Common workflow

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open My profile

    From the employee portal, open **Profile** in the section navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Choose a sub-area

    Navigate to **Personal**, **Emergency**, or **Banking** using the profile sub-nav or direct URL.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Save changes

    Submit the form. Resolve validation errors inline; successful saves refresh the snapshot on the page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Banking save asks me to sign in again">
    This is expected step-up behavior. Complete re-authentication, then retry save within the allowed window.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="A field is read-only">
    HR may lock fields after verification. Contact HR to change locked legal name or ID fields.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
