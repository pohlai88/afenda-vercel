## What is Org admin?

Org admin is the control plane for your Afenda organisation. It covers everything an organisation owner or administrator needs to govern access, review compliance, and configure workspace-wide settings.

## Capabilities

| Section | Description |
|---|---|
| **Members & roles** | Invite team members, assign roles (Member / Admin / Owner), and manage pending invitations |
| **Audit log** | Immutable chronological record of every data access and mutation across all modules |
| **Integrations** | Connect third-party services and configure webhooks for your organisation |
| **Settings** | Organisation name, timezone, branding, and data retention configuration |

## Who can access Org admin?

The Org admin area is accessible to members with the **Admin** or **Owner** role. Platform-level controls are reserved for the **Owner** role only.

To reach Org admin, open the left-hand rail and select **Admin**, or navigate directly to `/o/{orgSlug}/admin`.

## Audit trail

Every action taken in Org admin is logged to the immutable audit trail with the actor's identity, timestamp, and affected resource. The audit log is accessible under **Org admin → Audit** and is available for export.
