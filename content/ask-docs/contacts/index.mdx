## Overview

The **Contacts** module is your organisation's central address book under `/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/dashboard/contacts`. It stores people and companies, supports tags and segments, and links records to activities across the platform.

## Before you start

- You need an active org session and ERP permissions for contact operations.
- Creating or archiving contacts requires explicit ERP permission gates — not Better Auth org role alone.
- Bulk import uses CSV field mapping; validate a small sample before large uploads.

## Open Contacts

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Sign in and open Nexus at `/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/nexus`.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Select **Contacts** from the left-hand rail to open the directory list.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Use the search bar to find records by name, email, phone, or company. Save filtered views as named segments for quick reuse.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you can manage

| Capability | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **People** | Individual contacts with name, email, phone, company link, and custom fields |
| **Companies** | Organisation-level records linked to individual people |
| **Tags & segments** | Categorise and group contacts for filtered views |
| **Import** | Bulk-create contacts from CSV with field mapping |
| **Relationships** | Link contacts to each other (e.g. employee ↔ company) |

<Callout type="info" title="Permissions and audit">
  Contact mutations run through Server Actions guarded by `requireErpPermission`. Successful creates emit `erp.contact.record.create` audit events after commit.
</Callout>

## Search and filters

Combine multiple filters to narrow the list. Named segments persist your filter criteria so operators can return to the same view without re-entering conditions.

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Create or archive action is denied">
    Ask your administrator to grant the relevant ERP contact permissions for your role.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Import rows fail validation">
    Check required columns in the CSV template, deduplicate email addresses, and retry with a smaller batch to surface row-level errors.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
