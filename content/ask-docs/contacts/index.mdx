## Overview

The Contacts module is your organisation's central address book. It stores individuals and companies, links them to activities, and makes them searchable across the platform.

  Detailed guides for creating contacts, organising contacts, and bulk importing are being written and will appear in the sidebar shortly.
</Callout>

## What you can manage

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| **People** | Individual contacts with name, email, phone, company link, and custom fields |
| **Companies** | Organisation-level contact records linked to individual people |
| **Tags & segments** | Categorise and group contacts for quick filtered views |
| **Import** | Bulk-create contacts from a CSV file with field mapping |
| **Relationships** | Link contacts to each other (e.g. employee ↔ company) |

## Search and filters

Use the search bar at the top of the Contacts list to find records by name, email, phone, or company. Combine multiple filters to narrow results. All filtered views can be saved as named segments for quick access later.

## Access

The Contacts module is accessible from the left-hand rail in the Nexus workspace. Access to individual records and bulk operations is governed by your organisation's ERP permissions.
