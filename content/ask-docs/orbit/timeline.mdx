## Overview

**Orbit Timeline** (`/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/dashboard/orbit/timeline`) shows **upcoming, overdue, and scheduled pressure** across the execution horizon — beyond the single-day lens of [Today](/ask-docs/orbit/today).

Use Timeline when you need to answer: *what is coming, and what is already late?*

## Before you start

- You need an active org session and ERP permissions for the planner module.
- Timeline emphasises **Next** and overdue bands on Afenda's Past · Now · Next temporal spine.
- Items without schedule context may still appear when pressure rules elevate them (for example `blocked` or automation-attention states).

## Horizon semantics

Timeline organises work along Afenda's **Past · Now · Next** model:

| Band | Contents |
| --- | --- |
| **Overdue** | Scheduled work whose time window has passed without resolution |
| **Now** | Active and blocked execution in the current operating period |
| **Upcoming** | Scheduled items on future dates or recurrence instances |

## Clear overdue backlog

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Open **Timeline** at `/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/dashboard/orbit/timeline` and sort or filter to surface overdue items first.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    For each item, **reschedule**, **assign**, **unblock**, or **transition** toward review and verification.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Return to [Triage](/ask-docs/orbit/triage) to promote or correlate signals if overdue pressure originated as untriaged intake.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Plan the next period

Use Timeline with **Today** to commit work: schedule items from triage into concrete dates, then confirm the next seven to fourteen days look achievable.

<Callout type="info" title="Saved views">
  Persist sort and filter combinations as **saved views** (`erp.planner.view.upsert` / `erp.planner.view.delete`) — for example "overdue critical" or "unassigned next week".
</Callout>

## Recurrence and reminders

Recurring items and reminder policies appear on Timeline through their next instances. When automation fails, the failure also surfaces in [Triage](/ask-docs/orbit/triage) under automation attention.

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="An item is overdue but not on Timeline">
    Confirm the item has a schedule bound and is not resolved or cancelled. Pressure-only elevation still lists some blocked items without calendar dates.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Recurrence stopped generating instances">
    Check **Triage** under automation attention for failed reminder or recurrence runs before rescheduling manually.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Timeline and Today show different sets">
    **Today** filters to the current calendar day; **Timeline** spans the full horizon including overdue and future buckets.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
