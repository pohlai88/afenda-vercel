## Overview

**Orbit Today** (`/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/dashboard/orbit/today`) focuses on **scheduled and active operational work for the current day** — the operating window operators care about right now on the calendar.

Use Today when you need to answer: *what is supposed to happen today?*

## Before you start

- You need an active org session and ERP permissions for the planner module.
- **Today** emphasises calendar-day commitment; **Queue** emphasises immediate execution rank — an item can appear in both when it is high pressure and scheduled today.
- Scheduling changes emit planner audit events (`erp.planner.item.schedule`) and update how items appear on [Timeline](/ask-docs/orbit/timeline).

## What Today shows

Today aggregates items and pressure that intersect the **current day**, including:

- Items in **`scheduled`** or **`active`** lifecycle with a schedule bound to today
- Work that became due or started today
- Sessions and transitions that affect today's execution plan

Pressure ranking still applies within the day — critical blocked work may appear even when it was scheduled earlier.

## Working the day

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Open **Today** from the Orbit rail or command layer at `/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/dashboard/orbit/today`.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review the execution feed sorted by priority and schedule slack.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Select a row to inspect schedule, owner, and evidence in the detail pane.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Transition lifecycle states (activate, block, move to review) or **start a session** for focused execution.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Today vs Queue

| Surface | Emphasis |
| --- | --- |
| **Today** | Calendar day commitment — what the organisation planned for this date |
| **Queue** | Immediate execution rank — what must run now regardless of calendar bucket |

<Callout type="info" title="Move work across days">
  Use item scheduling actions from the detail pane to reschedule. Updates propagate to **Timeline** on the next request.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="An item scheduled for today is missing">
    Confirm lifecycle is `scheduled` or `active` and the schedule date matches the org timezone shown in the shell.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Blocked work dominates Today">
    High derived pressure elevates blocked items even when calendar-bound — unblock or reassign from the detail pane before rescheduling.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I need multi-day horizon view">
    Switch to [Timeline](/ask-docs/orbit/timeline) for overdue and upcoming bands beyond the current day.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
