## What is Orbit?

Orbit is Afenda's operational execution substrate. It brings together forward planning, signal detection, and structured delivery into a single workspace layer.

## Core concepts

| Concept | Description |
|---|---|
| **Signals** | Structured observations that indicate an item needs attention or action |
| **Planning items** | Forward-looking tasks with ownership, priority, and temporal status |
| **Execution** | Durable delivery runs tied to planning items — tracked to completion |

## Temporal model

Orbit organises work along a **Past · Now · Next** axis:

- **Past** — completed or archived items; the permanent record
- **Now** — items in active execution this period
- **Next** — committed items planned for upcoming periods

This model is embedded throughout the UI: filters, boards, and dashboards all surface items by temporal position.

## Access

Orbit is available from the left-hand rail in the Nexus workspace under **Orbit**. Access is governed by your organisation's ERP permissions. Contact your administrator if the module is not visible.
