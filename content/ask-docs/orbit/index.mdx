## Overview

**Orbit** is Afenda's operational execution substrate. It coordinates detected operational pressure (`PlannerSignal`), executable work (`PlannerItem`), and ERP-linked causality along a **Past · Now · Next** temporal spine.

Primary surfaces live under `/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/dashboard/orbit/*` — queue-first, not board-first.

## Before you start

- You need an active org session and ERP permissions for the planner module.
- Orbit appears in the left-hand rail from the Nexus workspace.
- Signals and items are separate primitives — promote pressure to items only when ownership and verification are required.

<Callout type="info" title="Product vs domain">
  **Orbit** is the product name; the internal module is **Planner** (`lib/features/planner/`). Documentation and UI use Orbit.
</Callout>

## Open Orbit

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Sign in and open your organisation Nexus at `/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/nexus`.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Select **Orbit** from the left-hand rail. The default landing surface is **Queue** (`/dashboard/orbit`).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Use surface tabs or navigation to move between Queue, Today, Timeline, Signals, Sessions, and Links.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Core concepts

| Concept | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Signals** | Structured operational pressure awaiting triage, promotion, or dismissal |
| **Planning items** | Executable work with ownership, schedule, and verification lifecycle |
| **Links** | Typed ERP attachments with mandatory causality reason |
| **Temporal model** | Past (archived) · Now (active) · Next (committed) |

## Primary surfaces

| Surface | Path segment | Use when |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Queue | `/dashboard/orbit` | Ranked execution — default entry |
| Today | `/dashboard/orbit/today` | Period-focused active work |
| Timeline | `/dashboard/orbit/timeline` | Temporal spine across periods |
| Signals | `/dashboard/orbit/signals` | Triage detected pressure |
| Sessions | `/dashboard/orbit/sessions` | Execution continuity and worklog |
| Links | `/dashboard/orbit/links` | ERP records tied to items |

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Orbit is missing from the rail">
    Your administrator must grant ERP planner permissions. Orbit is not visible from Better Auth org role alone.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I cannot promote a signal">
    Confirm the signal is not already correlated or dismissed. Promotion creates a `PlannerItem` and begins the execution lifecycle.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
