## Overview

**Summon Lynx** is the Nexus Field companion: a floating panel that runs **grounded truth retrieval** on whatever operational **atom** you have in focus on the canvas.

It is optimized for *while I am working on this record, what does our evidence say?* — not for full cross-module operator questions (use the [Lynx console](./truth-retrieval) for those).

## Opening summon

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Open **Nexus** (`/o/{orgSlug}`) and focus an item or record on the field.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click the **Summon Lynx** control (round Lynx brand mark in the shell).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    The panel shows **Grounded on** with the focused title, then streams truth for that context.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Drag the panel to reposition; use **Close** to dismiss. Keyboard and screen-reader labels use *Summon Lynx* / *Close Lynx*.

## Grounding behavior

| State | Behavior |
|---|---|
| **Atom in focus** | Truth runs (or refreshes) with a grounded query label tied to the focused entity |
| **No atom in focus** | Idle message: focus an item on the canvas to retrieve grounded truth |
| **Refresh** | Re-run retrieval after the underlying record changes |

Grounded truth uses the same evidence contract as the Lynx console:

- Answer
- Evidence
- Limitations
- Next safe action

## Copy and branding

Summon uses the **The Machine** eyebrow and Lynx title from `Dashboard.LynxSummon.*`. Status strings use *Working…* and *Retrieving grounded truth…* — not banned labels like *Processing* or *Generating*.

Icons use brand PNGs under `public/icons/lynx/` — not generic chat glyphs.

## Permissions

Summon inherits the same org session and Lynx access expectations as the Nexus shell. Users without Lynx workspace search cannot use ERP Lynx surfaces.
