## Overview

**Summon Lynx** is the Nexus Field companion: a floating panel that runs **grounded truth retrieval** on whatever operational **atom** you have in focus on the canvas at `/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/nexus`.

It answers *while I am working on this record, what does our evidence say?* — not full cross-module operator questions (use the [Lynx console](/ask-docs/lynx/truth-retrieval) for those).

## Before you start

- ERP permission **`lynx.workspace.search`** — summon calls `POST /api/erp/lynx/truth-search` via `#features/lynx/client`.
- Knowledge chunks with embeddings for the topics you expect to retrieve.
- An atom in focus on the Nexus field — summon idles without a focused entity.

<Callout type="info" title="Shell overlay">
  `LynxSummon` mounts from the org layout on all `/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/**` routes — the FAB persists while you navigate within the org shell.
</Callout>

## Opening summon

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Open **Nexus** at `/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/nexus` and focus an item or record on the field.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click the **Summon Lynx** control (round Lynx brand mark in the shell).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Read the **Grounded on** label, then review the streamed truth sections as retrieval completes.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Use deep links to **Knowledge substrate** or the **Lynx console** when you need chunk edits or operator tools.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Grounding behavior

| State | Behavior |
| --- | --- |
| **Atom in focus** | Truth runs (or refreshes) with a grounded query label tied to the focused entity |
| **No atom in focus** | Idle message — focus an item on the canvas to retrieve grounded truth |
| **Refresh** | Re-run retrieval after the underlying record changes |

Grounded truth uses the same four-block contract as the Lynx console: **Answer**, **Evidence used**, **Limitations**, and **Next safe action**.

## Copy and branding

Summon uses the **The Machine** eyebrow and Lynx title from `Dashboard.LynxSummon.*`. Status strings use *Working…* and *Retrieving grounded truth…* — not banned labels like *Processing* or *Generating*. Icons use brand PNGs under `public/icons/lynx/`.

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Summon control is missing">
    Confirm `lynx.workspace.search` and that you are inside the org shell layout (not a public or portal surface).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Panel says focus an item first">
    Select or focus an operational atom on the Nexus field — summon does not run ungrounded truth in this overlay.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Evidence is empty for a focused record">
    Add or expand chunks in [Knowledge substrate](/ask-docs/lynx/knowledge-substrate) covering that policy domain.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
