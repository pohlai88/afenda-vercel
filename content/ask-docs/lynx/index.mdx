## Overview

**Lynx** is Afenda's **machine layer** — not a generic chatbot or copilot. It resolves truth from your organisation's evidence, prepares work through structured layers, and recommends **read-only, governed** actions where policy allows.

Product copy uses **Lynx** and the eyebrow **The Machine**. Lynx composes the **Knowledge** substrate (pgvector chunks and embeddings); it does not replace ERP modules such as [Orbit](../orbit) or [HRM](../hrm).

## Where Lynx appears

| Surface | Audience | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| [Lynx console](./truth-retrieval) | Signed-in operators | Full module at `/o/{orgSlug}/dashboard/lynx` |
| [Nexus summon](./nexus-summon) | Signed-in operators | Grounded truth on the item in focus |
| [Ask Lynx on docs](./ask-docs-chat) | Public visitors | Product help from ask-docs content only |

ERP Lynx and **Ask Lynx** share branding but use **separate stacks** — never mix tenant ERP data into the public chat route.

## Four product layers

Lynx is organised into four layers (see [Core concepts](./concepts)):

1. **Truth retrieval** — evidence-grounded answers from knowledge chunks
2. **Operating briefs** — structured summaries (contract reserved; shipping incrementally)
3. **Canonical intake** — structured capture (`structured` in code; NL→SQL demo today)
4. **Decision operator** — governed read-only tools over org facts

## How to open the Lynx console

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Sign in and open your organisation workspace from **Nexus**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    In the left rail, select **Lynx** (`/o/{orgSlug}/dashboard/lynx`).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Use **Truth retrieval** for policy and process questions, **Operator assist** for cross-module read-only questions, and the **Knowledge substrate** section to add or debug chunks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Permissions and feature flags

- **Layout access:** ERP RBAC **`lynx.workspace.search`**
- **Operator assist:** `FLAG_LYNX_OPERATOR_ENABLED` (hidden with a policy notice when off)
- **Structured query demo:** `FLAG_LYNX_STRUCTURED_QUERY_DEMO_ENABLED`
- **Orbit operator tools:** `FLAG_LYNX_OPERATOR_ORBIT_TOOLS_ENABLED` (default on when operator is enabled)

Contact your administrator if Lynx is missing or sections show as disabled.

## Audit trail

ERP Lynx emits **`erp.lynx.*`** events, including:

| Action | Audit string |
|---|---|
| Truth query | `erp.lynx.truth.query` |
| Operator recommendation | `erp.lynx.operator.recommend` |
| NL→SQL demo query | `erp.lynx.nl_demo.query` |
| Brief generate (reserved) | `erp.lynx.brief.generate` |
| Intake commit (reserved) | `erp.lynx.intake.commit` |

Public **Ask Lynx** does not write IAM audit rows — it has no organisation session.
