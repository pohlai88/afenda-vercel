## Overview

Organisation members join Afenda through an email invitation. Each invitation carries a role that controls what the member can see and do across all modules. Only **Admin** or **Owner** members can send invitations.

## Sending an invitation

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open Members

    Go to **Org admin → Members** (`/o/{orgSlug}/admin/members`).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Initiate the invite

    Click **Invite member** and enter the invitee's email address.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Choose a role

    Select the appropriate role for the new member:

    | Role | Access level |
    |---|---|
    | **Member** | Standard workspace access — read and contribute within assigned modules |
    | **Admin** | Can manage members, integrations, and settings on behalf of the organisation |
    | **Owner** | Full control including destructive actions (delete org, transfer ownership) |

    <Callout type="warn" title="Role cannot be changed by the invitee">
      The invitee accepts the role you assign. Only an Admin or Owner can change a member's role after they join — members cannot self-promote.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Send the invitation

    Click **Send invitation**. The recipient receives an email with a time-limited invitation link. They must click the link and complete sign-up (or sign in if they already have an account) to join your organisation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Invitation limits

<Callout type="info" title="Rate limit">
  Afenda allows up to **30 invitations per hour** per organisation to prevent abuse. If you need to onboard a large team at once, spread invitations across multiple hours or contact support.
</Callout>

## Managing pending invitations

Pending invitations appear in the **Members** table with a `Pending` badge. You can:

- **Resend** — send the invitation email again if the recipient missed it
- **Revoke** — cancel the invitation before it is accepted

Both actions are available from the actions menu (`…`) next to each pending row.
