## Overview

Organisation members join Afenda through an email invitation. Each invitation carries a Better Auth organisation role that controls membership metadata. **ERP business authorization** is separate — assign tenant overlays and ERP permissions after members join.

Only members who can act as organisation **admin** or **owner** in Better Auth can send invitations from Org admin → Members.

## Before you start

- Tenant authority to open Org admin (`tenant_owner`, `tenant_key_admin`, or `tenant_support_admin`).
- The invitee's correct email address.
- A planned Better Auth role (Member, Admin, or Owner) — not a substitute for ERP RBAC.

<Callout type="info" title="Invitation rate limit">
  Afenda allows up to **30 invitations per hour** per organisation (`ORG_INVITE_MAX_PER_HOUR`). Spread bulk onboarding across multiple hours if needed.
</Callout>

## Sending an invitation

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open Members

    Go to **Org admin → Members** at `/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/admin/members`.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Initiate the invite

    Click **Invite member** and enter the invitee's email address.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Choose a role

    Select the appropriate Better Auth organisation role:

    | Role | Access level |
    | --- | --- |
    | **Member** | Standard membership — does not grant Org admin or ERP permissions by itself |
    | **Admin** | Can manage members and organisation settings in Better Auth |
    | **Owner** | Full membership control including destructive org actions |

    <Callout type="warn" title="Role cannot be self-promoted">
      The invitee accepts the role you assign. Only an Admin or Owner can change a member's role after they join.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Send the invitation

    Click **Send invitation**. The recipient receives an email with a time-limited link to `/{locale}/accept-invitation`. They complete sign-up or sign in, then join your organisation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing pending invitations

Pending invitations appear in the **Members** table with a `Pending` badge. From the row actions menu you can **Resend** or **Revoke** before acceptance. Successful invites emit `org.member.invite` audit events after commit.

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Invite button is missing">
    Confirm you hold Better Auth admin/owner membership and tenant authority for Org admin. ERP permissions alone do not unlock the Members UI.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Rate limit exceeded">
    Wait until the hourly window resets or contact your platform operator if you need a temporary exception for a large migration.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Invitee joined but cannot open HRM or Lynx">
    Assign ERP roles and `lynx.workspace.search` (and module permissions) on **Org admin → Access** — membership role is not business authorization.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
