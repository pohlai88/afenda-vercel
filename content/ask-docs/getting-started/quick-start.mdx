## Overview

This guide takes you through the three setup steps every new Afenda user completes: creating an account, creating an organisation, and reaching the Nexus workspace where all ERP modules live.

## Before you start

- A valid email address you control for verification.
- A modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari — latest two major versions).
- Your chosen organisation slug — permanent after creation.

## Setup steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Create your account

    Visit `/{locale}/sign-up` and enter your email address. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link. Set a strong password and complete your profile.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Create your organisation

    After email verification you land on the **console** loading bay at `/{locale}/console`. Afenda prompts you to create your first organisation:

    1. Enter your **organisation name** — this becomes your workspace URL slug (for example `acme`).
    2. Choose your **timezone** — used for attendance, leave, and payroll calculations.
    3. Click **Create organisation**.

    <Callout type="warn" title="Org slug is permanent">
      Your organisation slug (`acme` in `/en/o/acme/nexus`) cannot be changed after creation. Choose carefully — it appears in bookmarked URLs shared with your team.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Reach the Nexus workspace

    Once your organisation is created, Afenda redirects you to `/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/nexus` — the org root. From here you access every module via the left-hand rail.

    <Callout type="info" title="Bookmark your workspace">
      Afenda preserves your last position after sign-out via the `callbackUrl` parameter, so bookmarked pages return you to the same surface after sign-in.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Sign-up says email already exists">
    Use **Sign in** instead and complete verification if prompted. Invitation links also route through the auth shell.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Console shows no organisation">
    Complete the create-organisation form on the console loading bay. You need at least one organisation before Nexus is reachable.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Redirect lands on sign-in again">
    Confirm the session cookie is allowed in your browser and that you completed email verification.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
