## Overview

This guide takes you through the three setup steps every new Afenda user completes: creating an account, creating an organisation, and reaching the Nexus workspace where all modules live.

## Prerequisites

- A valid email address
- A modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari — latest two major versions)

## Setup steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Create your account

    Visit the Afenda sign-up page and enter your email address. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link. Set a strong password and complete your profile.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Create your organisation

    After email verification you land on the **console** loading bay. Afenda will prompt you to create your first organisation:

    1. Enter your **organisation name** — this becomes your workspace URL slug (e.g. `acme`).
    2. Choose your **timezone** — used for attendance, leave, and payroll calculations.
    3. Click **Create organisation**.

    <Callout type="warn" title="Org slug is permanent">
      Your organisation slug (`acme` in `/o/acme/`) cannot be changed after creation. Choose carefully — it appears in all bookmarked URLs shared with your team.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Reach the Nexus workspace

    Once your organisation is created, Afenda redirects you automatically to `/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/nexus` — the org root. From here you can access every module via the left-hand rail.

    <Callout type="info" title="Bookmark your workspace">
      Afenda preserves your last position after sign-out via the `callbackUrl` parameter, so any page you bookmark will bring you back to exactly where you were.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>
