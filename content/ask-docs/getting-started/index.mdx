## Overview

This section walks administrators and operators through first-week setup on Afenda: account creation, organisation bootstrap, Nexus orientation, and team invitations. Follow the guides in order for the fastest path to a working workspace.

## Before you start

- A valid email address you can verify immediately.
- A modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari — latest two major versions).
- Your intended organisation slug in mind — it is permanent after creation (for example `acme` in `/en/o/acme/nexus`).

<Callout type="info" title="Post-login loading bay">
  After verification, Afenda routes you to `/{locale}/console`. Single-org users redirect to Nexus automatically; multi-org users pick an active organisation first.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Verification email did not arrive">
    Check spam folders and retry from the sign-in page. Corporate filters may block automated mail — allowlist Afenda sender domains with your IT team.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Console does not redirect to Nexus">
    You may belong to zero organisations (create one) or multiple (select active org in the console loading bay).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Slug I want is unavailable">
    Organisation slugs are globally unique. Choose an alternate slug; display name can differ from the URL segment.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
