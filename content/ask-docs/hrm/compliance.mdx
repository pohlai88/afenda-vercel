## Overview

**Compliance** tracks statutory obligations per supported jurisdiction (e.g. MY, SG, ID, VN). Evidence rows capture generation state, hashes, and outbound delivery metadata so audits answer *who generated what, when, with which inputs*.

## Evidence packs

Packs bundle calculations, forms, and checksums. Regeneration invalidates prior hashes deliberately — never “silent overwrite” without a new evidence row.

## Aging & retries

Aging summaries highlight due-soon or overdue artefacts. Cron-assisted retries stay idempotent: if a pack already succeeded, reruns short-circuit without duplicating delivery.

<Callout type="info" title="Operator posture">
  Compliance screens prioritise honesty over cheerleading — expect explicit empty states when packs are not configured for the org’s jurisdictions.
</Callout>
