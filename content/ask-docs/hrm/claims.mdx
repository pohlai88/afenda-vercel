## Overview

**Claims** let employees submit reimbursable spend with typed line items and attachments. Approvers work a pending inbox; approved unpaid claims can feed the next payroll period so finance does not double-pay.

## Before you start

- Claim types are currency-aware — confirm policy limits before approvers work the queue.
- Evidence uploads use governed Blob storage — same contract as the **documents vault**.
- Portal users submit claims when portal access rows authorize them — not from guessed client state.

## Approving claims

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Open **HRM → Claims** and filter the pending approval inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review amount, policy hints, and evidence thumbnails for each submission.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Approve** to route unpaid rows toward payroll, or **deny** with a structured reason for resubmission.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    During payroll preparation, confirm approved unpaid claims appear in the hand-off summary.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Submission

Employees pick a claim type (currency-aware), describe the spend, and attach evidence stored in governed Blob storage. Drafts can be cancelled; submitted rows follow the approval machine.

## Approvals

Approvers see amount, policy hints, and evidence thumbnails. Denials return structured reasons so employees can resubmit with corrections.

<Callout type="info" title="Portal parity">
  The employee portal exposes claim submission for authenticated portal users — authority still resolves from portal access rows, not guessed client state.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Approved claim missing from payroll hand-off">
    Confirm the claim status is approved-unpaid and the target pay period is open for inclusion.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Evidence upload fails">
    Check file size limits and supported formats; retry from the portal or HRM evidence picker.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
