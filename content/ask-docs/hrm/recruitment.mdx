## Overview

Recruitment is the **internal ATS**: hiring managers publish requisitions, recruiters advance candidates through stages, and offers convert to employees when accepted. Every transition is validated server-side so impossible states cannot be saved.

## Requisitions

Draft requisitions capture title, department context, and headcount. Publishing locks the narrative for downstream pipeline work while still allowing controlled edits where policy permits.

## Applications & interviews

Applications link candidates to requisitions. Interviews capture outcomes that map to canonical event types for reporting — outcomes are enumerated, not free text.

## Offers

Offers carry compensation and start metadata. Approvals gate send actions; acceptance triggers conversion workflows that respect workforce uniqueness constraints.

<Callout type="warn" title="Separation of duties">
  Sensitive transitions may be denied when ERP RBAC detects conflicting duties for the same object family — prefer distinct approvers for create vs approve paths.
</Callout>
