## Overview

Recruitment is the **internal ATS**: hiring managers publish requisitions, recruiters advance candidates through stages, and offers convert to employees when accepted. Every transition is validated server-side so impossible states cannot be saved.

## Before you start

- Confirm **organization** departments and positions exist for requisition context.
- Sensitive transitions may require separation of duties — distinct approvers for create vs approve paths.
- Accepted offers respect workforce uniqueness constraints — duplicate employee numbers block conversion.

## Running a hiring pipeline

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Create a **requisition** with title, department context, and headcount — keep in draft until narrative is final.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Publish the requisition and link **applications** as candidates enter the pipeline.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Record **interview** outcomes using canonical event types (not free text) for reporting.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Route **offers** through approval, send to candidates, and convert to **employees** on acceptance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Requisitions

Draft requisitions capture title, department context, and headcount. Publishing locks the narrative for downstream pipeline work while still allowing controlled edits where policy permits.

## Applications & interviews

Applications link candidates to requisitions. Interviews capture outcomes that map to canonical event types for reporting — outcomes are enumerated, not free text.

## Offers

Offers carry compensation and start metadata. Approvals gate send actions; acceptance triggers conversion workflows that respect workforce uniqueness constraints.

<Callout type="warn" title="Separation of duties">
  Sensitive transitions may be denied when ERP RBAC detects conflicting duties for the same object family — prefer distinct approvers for create vs approve paths.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Offer conversion fails">
    Check for duplicate employee numbers or missing required contract fields on the offer payload.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Cannot publish a requisition">
    Department or headcount validation may be incomplete — open the draft and resolve flagged fields.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
