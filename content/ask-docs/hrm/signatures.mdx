## Overview

**Signatures** orchestrates multi-party electronic signature ceremonies inside Afenda. Signed envelopes attach to employment contracts, boarding tasks, and policy acknowledgements. Ceremonies are in-app — external e-sign vendors integrate only where explicitly configured.

Employees act on pending ceremonies from **HRM → Signatures** in the [employee portal](/ask-docs/portals/employee) when they are a party on the request.

## Workbench surface

Navigate to **HRM → Signatures** (`/o/{orgSlug}/dashboard/hrm/signatures`).

Administrators can:

- Create signature requests linked to HR artifacts
- Monitor party status (pending, completed, declined)
- Open ceremony detail by public slug for support

## Tier A — step-up authentication

Submitting a signature as a party may require **recent authentication step-up** (`requireRecentAuthStepUp`) before the ceremony completes. This protects high-impact HR documents from session hijack on shared machines.

## Permissions

| Action | ERP permission |
| --- | --- |
| Search and view ceremonies | `hrm.signature.search` |
| Create or manage requests | `hrm.signature.update` (and related keys per action) |

## Audits

Signature lifecycle events use `erp.hrm.signature.*` prefixes. Declines and completions are immutable business facts — use governed cancellation flows instead of deleting rows.
