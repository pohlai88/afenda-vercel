## Overview

**Signatures** orchestrates multi-party electronic signature ceremonies inside Afenda. Signed envelopes attach to employment contracts, boarding tasks, and policy acknowledgements. Ceremonies are in-app — external e-sign vendors integrate only where explicitly configured.

Employees act on pending ceremonies from **HRM → Signatures** in the [employee portal](/ask-docs/portals/employee) when they are a party on the request.

## Before you start

- Confirm the underlying HR artifact (contract, boarding task, policy version) exists before creating a ceremony.
- Parties must have active workforce or portal access to complete their signing step.
- High-impact completions may require **recent authentication step-up** — ensure signers can complete step-up on the device they use.

## Creating and monitoring ceremonies

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Navigate to **HRM → Signatures** (`/o/{orgSlug}/dashboard/hrm/signatures`).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Create a signature request linked to the HR artifact (contract, boarding task, or policy acknowledgement).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Monitor party status (pending, completed, declined) from the workbench list or ceremony detail by public slug.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    After all required parties complete, store the signed envelope evidence in the **documents vault** when your process requires retention.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tier A — step-up authentication

Submitting a signature as a party may require **recent authentication step-up** (`requireRecentAuthStepUp`) before the ceremony completes. This protects high-impact HR documents from session hijack on shared machines.

<Callout type="warn" title="Step-up on completion">
  If a signer sees a session-expired or step-up interruption, they must re-authenticate and return to the pending ceremony — partial saves do not count as signed.
</Callout>

## Permissions

| Action | ERP permission |
| --- | --- |
| Search and view ceremonies | `hrm.signature.search` |
| Create or manage requests | `hrm.signature.update` (and related keys per action) |

## Audits

Signature lifecycle events use `erp.hrm.signature.*` prefixes. Declines and completions are immutable business facts — use governed cancellation flows instead of deleting rows.

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Party cannot open the ceremony">
    Confirm they are listed on the request, portal access is active, and the ceremony has not already completed or been declined.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Completion blocked by step-up">
    Ask the signer to complete recent authentication, then retry from the pending ceremony link.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
