## Overview

The HRM module covers the full employee lifecycle — from the moment someone joins your organisation to offboarding, with compliance and audit coverage throughout.

In the Workbench, HRM is **registry-driven**: each sidebar segment maps to a row in `HRM_CAPABILITIES` (including bulk **HR import** for governed CSV sessions when that capability is enabled). What you see in the product nav matches that registry, ERP RBAC keys, and audit prefixes — so capabilities stay aligned as the module grows.

## Permissions

Access to HRM capabilities is controlled by ERP RBAC. Your organisation administrator assigns permissions per role. If you cannot see a section, contact your admin.

## Data protection

All employee data processed in HRM is treated as sensitive. Afenda logs every data access and mutation to the immutable audit trail. See [Audit log](../org-admin) for details.
