## Overview

The HRM module covers the full employee lifecycle — from the moment someone joins your organisation to offboarding, with compliance and audit coverage throughout.

In the Workbench, HRM is **registry-driven**: each sidebar segment maps to a row in `HRM_CAPABILITIES` (including bulk **HR import** for governed CSV sessions when that capability is enabled). What you see in the product nav matches that registry, ERP RBAC keys, and audit prefixes — so capabilities stay aligned as the module grows.

## Before you start

- Sign in to the Workbench with an org membership and an ERP role that includes the HRM segments you need.
- Confirm **organization structure** (departments, positions, grades) before bulk employee creation or imports.
- Assign ERP permissions through your organisation administrator — Better Auth org roles (`admin` / `owner`) do not replace HRM business authorization.

## Getting started in HRM

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Open **HRM → Employees** and create or import your workforce master records.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Configure **HRM → Organization** so placements and reporting lines resolve correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Enable operational modules (attendance, leave, payroll) as your rollout requires — each segment is gated by its own ERP permission keys.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Review the **HR snapshot** for operational pressure and deep links into execution surfaces.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Data protection

All employee data processed in HRM is treated as sensitive. Afenda logs every data access and mutation to the immutable audit trail. See [Org admin](/ask-docs/org-admin) for governance surfaces.

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="A sidebar segment is missing">
    Your ERP role may lack the permission keys for that capability. Ask an administrator to review effective permissions in org admin settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Actions return access denied">
    Business authorization resolves from ERP RBAC, not org membership role alone. Confirm the specific `hrm.*` keys for the workflow you are running.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
