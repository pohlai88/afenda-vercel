## Overview

The leave management system handles the full lifecycle of employee time off: request submission, manager approval, balance tracking, and policy enforcement. Leave types and accrual rules are configured by your organisation administrator.

## Leave types

Your organisation administrator configures the leave types available to employees, for example:

- Annual leave
- Sick leave
- Maternity / paternity leave
- Unpaid leave

Each type carries its own **accrual policy**, **carryover rules**, and **approval workflow**.

## Submitting a leave request

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Navigate to **HRM → Leave**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Request leave**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Select the **leave type**, **start date**, and **end date**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Add an optional note for your manager, then click **Submit**.

    Your manager receives a notification to review the request. Leave balance is not deducted until the request is approved.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Approval workflow

Managers see pending requests in their **Leave** queue. Three outcomes are available:

| Action | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Approve** | Leave is confirmed; balance is deducted; employee is notified. |
| **Reject** | Request is declined; employee is notified with the reason. |
| **Request changes** | Employee is asked to adjust dates before resubmission. |

## Leave balances

Each employee's current balances are shown on their profile under **Leave balances**. Balances update in real time when requests are approved or reversed.

## Carry-over and expiry

Leave balances configured with a carry-over limit will automatically expire unused days at the end of the leave year.

<Callout type="info" title="Advance expiry notice">
  Employees receive a notification ahead of the expiry date (configurable advance window, default: 30 days) so they have time to use remaining days or raise an exception with their manager.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info" title="Portal parity">
  Employees submit and cancel **pending** leave at [Employee portal leave](/ask-docs/portals/leave). Managers approve in this HRM module.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="Employee cannot see a leave type">
    Confirm the type is **active** for the organisation and the employee's population is in scope for that policy.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Balance did not update after approval">
    Refresh the employee profile balances card; if still wrong, check for parallel unapproved requests consuming reserved balance.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
