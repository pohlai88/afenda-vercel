## Overview

The documents vault provides a secure, per-employee file store for compliance-critical documents. Every upload is access-controlled, logged to the audit trail, and monitored for expiry by an automated watch process.

## Document types stored in the vault

- Employment contracts
- Right-to-work evidence
- Certifications and licences
- Signed policy acknowledgements
- Appraisal records

## Uploading a document

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Open the relevant **employee profile** and click the **Documents** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Upload document**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Select the **document type** from the dropdown and upload the file (PDF, DOCX, or image formats accepted).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Set an **expiry date** if the document has a validity period (e.g. right-to-work evidence, certifications).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Save**. The document is stored and immediately visible on the employee's profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Expiry tracking

<Callout type="warn" title="Don't miss expiries">
  Afenda's **document expiry watch** cron job runs daily and sends notifications to the employee's manager and HR administrators when a document is approaching its expiry date. The default advance notice window is **30 days** — configure this in your HRM settings.
</Callout>

Documents past their expiry date display an `Expired` badge in the vault and in the **Compliance dashboard** under **HRM → Compliance**.

## Access control

<Callout type="info" title="Permissions required">
  Viewing employee documents requires the `hrm.documents.read` ERP permission. Uploading requires `hrm.documents.create`. All accesses — reads and writes — are logged to the audit trail with the accessor's identity and timestamp.
</Callout>

Contact your organisation administrator if you need permission adjustments.
