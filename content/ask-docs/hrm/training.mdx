## Overview

**Training & development** covers the full learning loop: catalog courses, schedule sessions, assign employees, record completions, and track recertification deadlines. Compliance-sensitive training can bridge to statutory evidence where your jurisdiction pack requires it.

Employees view assignments and submit feedback from the [employee portal](/ask-docs/portals/employee) **My training** section when portal access is active.

## Workbench surface

Navigate to **HRM → Training & development** (`/o/{orgSlug}/dashboard/hrm/training`).

| Area | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Course catalog | Define courses, duration, and renewal rules |
| Sessions | Schedule classroom or virtual sessions with capacity |
| Assignments | Attach employees to courses or sessions |
| Completions | Record pass/fail and evidence |
| Recertification | Surface upcoming expiries for compliance follow-up |

## Permissions

| Action | ERP permission |
| --- | --- |
| View catalog, sessions, and assignments | `hrm.training.search` |
| Create or update training artifacts | `hrm.training.update` |

## Audits

Training mutations write `erp.hrm.training.*` events. Completion records are durable — correct mistakes through governed reversal flows rather than deleting audit history.

<Steps>
  <Step>Create or select a course in the catalog.</Step>
  <Step>Schedule a session (optional) or assign directly to employees.</Step>
  <Step>Record completion when evidence is received.</Step>
  <Step>Monitor recertification widgets on the compliance or snapshot surfaces.</Step>
</Steps>
