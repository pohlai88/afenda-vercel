## Overview

Afenda records attendance at the individual shift level. Every record links to an employee, captures clock-in and clock-out timestamps, and flags anomalies automatically so managers can act without manually reviewing every row.

## What each record captures

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Employee** | The person the record belongs to |
| **Clock-in** | Timestamp and method (manual, kiosk, or integration) |
| **Clock-out** | Timestamp; missing clock-out is an automatic anomaly flag |
| **Hours worked** | Computed from in/out timestamps |
| **Anomaly flags** | Late arrival, early departure, missing clock-out, shift deviation |

## Viewing attendance records

Go to **HRM → Attendance**. Use the date range picker and employee filter to narrow the view. Click **Export CSV** in the toolbar to download the filtered data.

## Making a manual correction

Managers with the `hrm.attendance.update` ERP permission can correct records.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click a record row to open its detail panel.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Edit the **clock-in** or **clock-out** time as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Add a **correction note** — a clear explanation of why the change was made. This note is stored with the correction and visible in the audit trail.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click **Save**. The correction is committed with your user identity, the original value, and the updated value logged to the audit trail.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warn" title="Corrections are audited">
  Every manual correction writes an immutable audit event (`hrm.attendance.record.update`). The correction note is required and cannot be blank.
</Callout>

## Anomaly watch

The **HRM compliance aging watch** cron job runs daily and automatically flags:

- Employees with missing attendance records for a working day
- Consecutive absences without an approved leave request
- Clock-in times outside the expected shift window (configurable threshold)

Flagged anomalies appear in the **Compliance** tab and generate notifications to the employee's manager.
