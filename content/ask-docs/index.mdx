## Overview

Afenda is an integrated ERP platform for modern organisations. It brings together HRM, operational execution (Orbit), contacts, org administration, and Lynx — Afenda's machine layer for evidence-grounded answers — in a single locale-first workspace at `/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/…`.

This documentation site (`/{locale}/ask-docs`) is public and does not require sign-in. Signed-in operators use the same product surfaces described here inside the Workbench shell.

## Before you start

- Use a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari — latest two major versions).
- Bookmark your organisation Nexus at `/{locale}/o/{orgSlug}/nexus` after first sign-in.
- For tenant-specific policy answers, use **ERP Lynx** inside the workspace — not the public **Ask Lynx** chat on this site.

<Callout type="info" title="Locale-first URLs">
  Every public and Workbench URL includes a locale prefix (for example `/en/ask-docs` or `/en/o/acme/nexus`). Bare `/o/…` or `/ask-docs/…` paths are invalid in bookmarks and emails.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions type="single">
  <Accordion title="I cannot find my organisation after sign-in">
    Complete email verification, then land on `/{locale}/console`. If you belong to multiple organisations, use the utility-bar org switcher after sign-in.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Search returns no results">
    Confirm you are on the correct locale (`/en/ask-docs`, `/zh-CN/ask-docs`, etc.). Orama search indexes processed MDX for configured locales.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Ask Lynx cannot answer a tenant-specific question">
    Public Ask Lynx has no org session. Sign in and use ERP [Truth retrieval](/ask-docs/lynx/truth-retrieval) or [Operator assist](/ask-docs/lynx/operator-assist) for live tenant data.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
