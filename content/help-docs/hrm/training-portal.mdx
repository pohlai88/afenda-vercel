# My training

Employees open **Portal → My training** to see open assignments and attest completion. HR may verify self-attested records in the admin workbench.

Upload certificates using the standard HRM document Blob path contract (`/orgs/{orgId}/hrm/{employeeId}/...`) when attaching evidence to a completion.
