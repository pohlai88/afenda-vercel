# Training & development

Use **Dashboard → HRM → Training & development** to maintain the course catalog, schedule sessions, assign mandatory training, record completions, and verify employee attestations.

## Workflow

1. **Categories** — optional grouping for reporting.
2. **Courses** — reusable definitions (delivery mode, statutory flag, recertification interval).
3. **Sessions** — scheduled occurrences with rosters; **Close session** completes all present attendees in one transaction.
4. **Assignments** — open work for employees (manual, onboarding, or recertification).
5. **Records** — completion history; HR can verify self-attested portal submissions.

Statutory courses (`statutoryFlag`) emit compliance evidence rows for the aging-watch pipeline. Completing a course linked from an onboarding boarding task (`metadata.trainingCourseCode`) auto-completes that task.
